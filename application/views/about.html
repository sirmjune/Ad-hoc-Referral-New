ON AD-HOC REFERRAL

Ad-Hoc-Referral is an IT based solution Company providing support to clients and linking them to adversity of professionals Medical, Engineering, Architectural, Environment and Social services through the Ad-Hoc Web site and the Ad-Hoc Application platforms.

VISION

To become the region’s best referral platform aiding and accessing clients to the best medical and other professional support across the East African Region and the World, bridging businesses for a profit.



MISSION STATEMENT

To provide the most effective, readily and easily usable platforms to clients to access professional support across a wide range of professions.



CORE VALUES

• Transparency. • Accountability. • Empathy. • Respect. • Trust.
